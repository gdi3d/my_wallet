My Wallet
Keep track of your money

What is it?

A small and simple income/outcome money app written in python using Django

It's RESTful, and has a very simple interface to play with

Use it like this

Create a Wallet.
Just a place to track (bank, pockets, socks)
Enter Your Transactions.
Add your income/outcome of your wallets.
You can add a category, date and note
See the history
Browse trough your wallet's history
